About me

I'm Trisha Bedi, a graphic design student based in Melbourne. I'm drawn to work that sits between concept and craft — where an idea is strong enough to drive every visual decision.

My practice moves across branding, publication design, and illustration. I'm especially interested in how type and image talk to each other, and how design can make complicated things feel clear.

Outside the studio I'm usually into fashion, jewellery, physical arts like crochet & clay making, reading design criticism, or taking photos. Currently in my final year at RMIT University.
